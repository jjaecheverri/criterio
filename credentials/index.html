How Verification Works
01
Create your account
Sign up with your professional email. Include your title, organization, and years of experience. Google sign-in available.
02
Confirm your identity
We verify your LinkedIn profile or professional email domain to confirm your industry credentials. Most verifications complete in under 24 hours.
03
Access the peer layer
Once verified, you can submit structured assessments, add typed annotations, and build your professional peer review record.
04
Build your level
Every assessment you submit builds your GROUND level — from Signal Watcher to GROUND Expert. Your assessments become part of the permanent peer record.
