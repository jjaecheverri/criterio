GROUND is a professional intelligence platform for professionals in real estate, retail, and brand strategy.

What is GROUND?

GROUND publishes professional-validated industry research signals — structured analyses of market dynamics, behavioral shifts, and structural patterns across three verticals: Real Estate, Retail & E-Commerce, and Brand & Travel.

Every article begins as an AI-generated analysis (Level 0). As professionals with domain expertise validate and enrich each signal, the article levels up — integrating real-world intelligence into a progressively stronger research artifact.

This is not crowd-sourced opinion. GROUND validation requires professional credentials and substantive input. The result is a research corpus that combines AI pattern recognition with human expertise.

Validation Stacking

The GROUND validation system is called stacking. Each validation layer adds depth, corrects gaps, and integrates professional context — making each subsequent level more authoritative than the last.

Validate Research

If you have professional expertise in real estate, retail, or brand strategy, you can validate GROUND signals in your domain. Validators are credited on every article they level up.

Validation means: reading the AI-generated analysis, providing corrections, additions, and professional context from your direct experience, and submitting your assessment. The AI integrates your input and revises the article in real time.
